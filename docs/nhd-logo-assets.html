Logo Usage Guidelines

Clear Space Rules

  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "N" in North
  • No elements should encroach the protection zone
  • Keep logos isolated from other graphics and text

Minimum Sizes

  • Digital: 120px width minimum for horizontal logo
  • Print: 1 inch (25mm) width minimum
  • Favicon/App Icon: 16x16px using symbol only
  • Social Media Profile: 400x400px minimum

Don'ts

  • Don't stretch or distort the logo
  • Don't change colors outside brand palette
  • Don't add effects like shadows or outlines
  • Don't place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Don't recreate or modify the logo
  • Don't rotate or tilt the logo
